About the App

Unleash Your Creativity with "Who's Talking Now"! Have you ever wished to bring your photos to life with a little personality? With "Who's Talking Now," you can do just that and more! Imagine transforming a simple snapshot into a delightful conversation.

This FREE app allows you to attach your thoughts in a whimsical speech balloon to any photo from your Photos Library. Whether it's a hilarious caption for a funny face or a heartfelt message for a cherished memory, your creativity knows no bounds! Share your unique creations instantly through Messages and watch as friends and family can't resist joining the fun.
